1. Overview

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information across our sports management platform and our integrated retail store. By using our services, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

2. Information We Collect

  • Player & Staff Data: Names, biometric data, performance statistics, and professional history recorded for league and team management.
  • Gallery Media: Photography and videography captured during official events, displayed in our interactive carousels.
  • Store Information: Shipping addresses, contact details, and order history for purchases made through our Store App.
  • Technical Data: IP addresses, browser types, and cookie identifiers used for website analytics and dashboard performance.

3. Use of Interactive Media & Statistics

Our website utilizes interactive tools (such as Chart.js and Owl Carousel) to visualize player performance. Data displayed in these charts is derived from official game records. Media displayed in our galleries is used for promotional purposes and to highlight athlete achievements.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal information. We may share data with:

  • Service providers assisting in payment processing for the Store.
  • Database hosting services for our sports statistics.
  • Legal authorities if required by law to protect our rights or the safety of our players.

5. Cookies and Analytics

We use cookies to enhance your experience, remember your dashboard preferences, and manage your shopping cart in the Store App. You may disable cookies in your browser settings, though some website features may stop functioning.

6. Your Rights & Corrections

As a user or athlete, you have the right to request access to the data we hold about you. If you notice inaccuracies in your Game Summary Reports or Personal Profiles, please contact the System Administrator to make corrections before the final season sync.
